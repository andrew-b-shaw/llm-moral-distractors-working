Who loves the sun? You, all your life, but as you know in the North, in Picardy the sun is not often there. As usual, before going to bed you looked at the weather: sunny with intermittent clouds and 25 degrees Celsius. After seeing this good news you went to your wardrobe and took out shorts, a floral t-shirt and a pair of sandals. After hanging your vest on your coat door you went to bed. You could finally close your eyes in tranquility, and dream of the day that would await you. Unfortunately, when you woke in the morning you had the bad surprise that it was raining and that you could not distinguish or even expect any rays of sun for the whole day. Therefore, annoyed, you had to go back to your wardrobe, store your clothes you took out the day before, to get out the warmest ones to face this day; you went out carrying an umbrella.
