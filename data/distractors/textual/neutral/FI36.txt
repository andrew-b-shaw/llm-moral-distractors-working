You open the fridge and look for something to eat. You feel like eating something light, but tasty. There is a carton of milk in the door and a packet of margarine on the upper shelf. In the vegetable box, there are some tomatoes and a cucumber you just bought. You take the milk, margarine and vegetables to the kitchen counter and close the fridge. You reach up on your tiptoes to get the granola box and bread from the cupboard, then place them on the counter as well. You cut a few thick slices of bread and put them in the toaster, then put the rest of the loaf in a bag. While the bread is in the toaster, you cut some tomato and cucumber slices. You get a bowl from the cupboard and pour granola and milk into it. When the bread is toasted, you put the bread on a plate and spread margarine and the sliced vegetables on them. You carry the granola and sandwich to the table and sit down to enjoy your meal.
