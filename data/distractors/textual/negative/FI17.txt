You join your friends in loud applause when the last band finishes playing. Part of the audience is still in a lively party mood when you start zigzagging your way through the crowd toward the exit. You walk toward the gates, and little by little, fewer people are around. You see in the distance a big, drunken man stumbling toward you. You don’t pay attention to him because you’re so involved in your conversation. Suddenly, the man is right next to you, and he falls against you. He smells like cigarettes and booze, and you jump away from him. You cannot hold back a sneer. The man is stumbling around, his eyes half closed, then he suddenly throws up on you. You wince in horror as the foul-smelling vomit splashes on you. It sprays on the front of your coat and on your jeans. You feel nauseous, and you feel your own vomit raising in your throat. The man’s yellow vomit drips from your coat onto your shoes and your bare hand.
