You work at a company that cleans rental cabins. You’re on your way to clean a cabin that the previous tenants failed to clean properly before leaving a couple of weeks ago. You have a bad feeling about this because tenants often leave these cabins in terrible shape. You arrive at the cabin, and a rotten smell hits you as you open the door. You stop and let out a long, deep breath, then close your eyes as you start to feel nauseous. You make yourself step inside. You try to hold your breath as you walk into the kitchen. A trash bin has fallen to the floor, and its contents have spilled onto the floor, staining the carpet. Maggots are crawling over the leftover food. You try not to think about what the other rooms must look like. You just know that you need to clean up this mess.
