You are angry at whoever made some days seem so long. Although they all have the same number of hours, there are some days that seem longer than usual. Today is one of them. As early as you woke up, you felt you should not have woken up. Your legs hurt and you do not feel like dragging yourself to the same coffee bar chair where you pretend that your life is good. The breathing of the man at the table next to you irritates you. It makes you dig your nails into the wood of the table and scratch it as if your nails were steel. Not to mention the noise that he makes when chewing that cake that he asks for every Wednesday. You can hear every detail of that man's digestive process. His mouth opening, his teeth grinding and chewing, his saliva mixing with the bits of food. It feels like your hearing has been amplified. Every little noise, imperceptible to other human beings, annoys you. It makes your brain boil, your feet jump constantly and your hands close in clenched fists. Involuntarily, you punch the table top whilst you shout at the man in question, "Can't you hear yourself?" He is perplexed. He does not utter a single word. He puts down the cup of coffee, the half-cake, pays the bill, and leaves. He never comes through that door again.
