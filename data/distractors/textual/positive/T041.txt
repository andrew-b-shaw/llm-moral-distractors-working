The place is the MEB Sports Hall ... You and one of your best friends, Cem, have a match against your old club. You could not play in the match because you injured your heel in a stupid match before. You wanted to play this match so much that you cried when your friends took their position on the court. You had a good start and took the lead. At half time, you went to the locker room in advance. When the team returned from the locker room, you had the same opponent but a different referee team. In the very beginning, you could not get how your two coaches were sent off without reason. Twelve teenagers, 15 years old, felt like fish out of water. You were running to the hall, taking your coach's commands and conveying them clumsily to your friends. There were players who had cramps, who injured themselves to get the ball. Unbelievable motivation. You haven't seen such a dedicated and connected twelve players. The match nearly came to a draw but for your coaches and yourselves: you pushed your limits and won the match. In the end you were so happy. It was an extraordinary youth match. After that you saw that referee team four times and they conducted the matches appropriately.
