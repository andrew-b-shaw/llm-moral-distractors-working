You love it when you're at home on a summer evening and a violent thunderstorm starts suddenly. Outside it gets cold, wet and uncomfortable. Especially on weekends you take the thunderstorm as an occasion to treat yourself to a few really cozy hours. You make yourself a hot chocolate that fills the whole living room with a delicious smell. Then you push the comfortable armchair close to the window and muffle yourself into a cuddly blanket. This way you can watch the storm outside safe and protected. From time to time, you sip your chocolate and feel how the pleasant sweetness spreads warmly in your body. When it gets dark, you light candles and listen to the sound of the rain. But it gets really heavenly when your cat decides not to hide from the thunder anymore and to share the cozy hours with you. When he rolls up on your lap, purring, you stroke his soft fur and enjoy life while the world outside is washed clean.
