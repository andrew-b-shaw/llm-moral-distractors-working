The alpine herbs smell wonderfully fresh in the morning hours. Place the paraglider on the cool grass and start running. Then it's like a stroke of a bird's wing and you … float. You look east to the Finsteraarhorn and west to the Matterhorn and feel in awe of these white peaks, above them the blue of the sky. You reach out with your arms and embrace the whole world with happiness. An eagle flies directly toward you. Just in front of you it veers away, flies to your left wing and we turn our circles. Your heart bounces – flying with an eagle means bliss to you. The eagle swings up faster to new heights and you see the red peaks in the south. The forest shines in the sun's rays from yellow to light green to dark green. Time to land – you look at your meadow and land gently like an angel on Earth. What remains is the wonderful feeling, a flight – an unforgettable, fascinating experience.
