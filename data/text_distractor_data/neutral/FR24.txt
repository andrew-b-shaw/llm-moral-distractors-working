On leaving college with your friends, you noticed that the buses were no longer running. This disruption was due to the arrival of the President of the Republic in your charming city. So you decided to go home with your friends, since you lived more than three miles away. You were in the middle of winter and yet there was a beautiful sun and the temperatures were unusually mild, which made the trip rather enjoyable. Moreover, as there were several of you, you chatted and laughed, so that the journey passed quickly. Once you arrived in the city center, it was time for you to separate, because you lived in very different places. You found yourself alone, thinking that the rest of the journey would henceforth be more painful. But you were totally mistaken. You passed through little streets that you did not know, along the river. You felt like you was rediscovering your city. It was a nice walk.
