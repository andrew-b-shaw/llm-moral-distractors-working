The Maldives are beautiful. You are sitting in a boat with a dozen fellow divers; in order to, in a couple of seconds, take the first vacation dive in the water with your dear wife. Slowly you glide down to a depth of 30 meters, where the dive guide takes you into a fascinating underwater world. You dive on a reef with medusas, corals and a lot of small colorful fish, as the guide signals you that you, the last ones still in water, should surface independently. Then he disappears and you have the sea all to yourselves when it suddenly gets dark. Baffled, you look up and see three huge manta rays which sail elegantly through the water and make a looping right in front of you. Wow – every diver's dream has come true. This experience of shared joy makes you smile even decades later. Thank you, dear mantas, for this unforgettable moment!
