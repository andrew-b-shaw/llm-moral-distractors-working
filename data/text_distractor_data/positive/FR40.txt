Autumn and pleasures. You enjoy a hike: Pleasures for the senses this morning, pleasure for the eyes this trip, while nothing is decided yet, and you walk in the sunny undergrowth. What surprises will there be on this beautiful day! What do you see there? What makes you think nature has awoken? What aroma do you sense? Could you imagine it? Under golden leaves chubby, well-wrapped and completely hidden, five pretty porcini mushrooms, kings of the forest, were hiding. You start to dream about your dinner: Oh beauty, oh the beautiful dish! Well-colored, probably well-packed, but the great feast of your hike, well exceeding your expectations of your morning. Not only did you look at your trophy, you admired their layout, you guessed their velvet feel, you already smelled your beautiful plate in your old fireplace: in short, your eyes were almost imprisoned by such a beauty born on a morning of such a lovely day.
