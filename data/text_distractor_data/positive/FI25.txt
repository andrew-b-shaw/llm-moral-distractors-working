It’s a sunny day in the mountains, and you’ve just taken a ski lift to the top. The sky is bright blue, and the sun is warming your face. It’s a perfect temperature for skiing. You stop for a moment to take in the wintry scenery. The snow-topped mountain peaks stretch toward the horizon. There are no trees at these altitudes, just some old, dead pines here and there. You see a ski hut at the bottom of the slope and decide to go there. The thought of a steaming cup of hot chocolate makes your mouth water. You push yourself in motion and feel fantastically free when you slide down the slope. You first gather some speed, then make a controlled turn, which slows me down a little. Your movements are relaxed and effortless. Powdery snow whirls behind me as you speed up again after the turn. You zigzag smoothly down the hill, the wind caressing your face.
