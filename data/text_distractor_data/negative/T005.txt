Everything happened on a regular day at March. Surely it would be a good day because they were serving roast beef at lunch! (If you are not a student struggling to get by, you won't understand what this means). But something happened and you ran late to lunch. Even though your friend insisted you chose the line, she knows you didn't, because you believe that other line was cursed. You couldn't believe your eyes: they were out of beef and serving chicken instead. Cold, leftover chicken. You bowed to your bad luck and took your chicken, and found a table. That day, that table was the first time you saw him. But neither of you was aware of that. You were about to start your lunch: "Wait, what's that?" They had started to serve beef again! Now this is a combination of tragedy, regret and despair. So unfair and so cold. But if you had another chance, if you knew that you would be meeting him, you would choose the same line. Some regrets in life should never happen, dear friends. If you have a chance to choose beef over chicken, take the beef. Otherwise you will regret it.
