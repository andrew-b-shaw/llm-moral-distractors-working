You had a day off and you decided to go out and take a walk in the woods. You didn't know the area, and you found an old building that seemed abandoned. As you entered the house, you felt like somebody was watching you. As you walked into a room you felt a chill, a distant silhouette was emerging through the only window, and you heard a humming sound that was gradually getting louder. As your stomach squeezed, a gust of wind snapped the door of a wardrobe. Next to it was a mirror, and with horror you saw that someone was standing behind you. Completely paralyzed, you watched him go through you, then disappear. When you were going home, you noticed that nobody answered your hellos or looked at you. As you went to bed, passing in front of your mirror, you had an intense sensation of uneasiness: the only reflection you saw was that of this someone that had just taken you from your existence, your own life.
