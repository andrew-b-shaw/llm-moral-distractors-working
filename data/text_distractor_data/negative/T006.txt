You were lucky enough to do your mandatory internship in a firm that offers a minimum wage. The firm said that you would get your payment at the 5th of each month. So, you decided to offer your friends a meal on the 6th. You told them you would spend your first salary together, and that made them very happy. On the 6th, you prepared: a friend picked you up with his car, seven of you including your girlfriend and you, to go to a restaurant. You ordered almost everything on the menu, tasted everything. Even so you ordered dessert (which is a rare event for a student, unless you are the child of a member of parliament or such like). You ate until you are completely full, then asked for the check. You gave your credit card to the waiter, entered your PIN and waited. The waiter said, "Sir, not enough balance." you were shocked: "How come? you just got paid, try again," and he did, but again not enough balance. All of you paid your checks separately, and if that was not enough, they even had to pay yours, because you had no money. Later you found out they weren't paying the interns.
