Your roommate isn't one of those guys who values cleanliness and order. He leaves his dirty clothes everywhere, never rinses the dishes and you don't even want to start with the stench that comes out of his room. But what he has achieved today caps it all off. You both spent the Easter holidays with your parents, so your apartment was empty. Your roommate left one day after you and you came back one day before him. As you entered your apartment, a penetrating smell of decomposition wafted toward you and the air was filled with fat, humming flies. The moron had left an open pack of minced meat standing in the kitchen in summery temperatures! And even with the window tilted open! When you saw this teeming mass of decomposing flesh and the white maggot bodies, you vomited into the sink in a flood. You're staying at a friend's place tonight. You'll leave the cleaning up to the "fly breeder."
